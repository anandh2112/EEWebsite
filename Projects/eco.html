C Qu I C C

Client:

Centre for Quantum Information, Communication, and Computing (CQuICC)

Location:

Chennai

Size:

Approx. 10,000 sqft

Project Overview

At Elements Energy, we embarked on designing India's first state-of-the-art Quantum Computing Laboratory and office space, aiming to blend cutting-edge technology with sustainable practices. The lab's design reflects a futuristic vision while ensuring energy efficiency and material sustainability.

3.A Futuristic Design Concept

The lab interiors reflect a futuristic theme characterized by:

  • A sharp contrast between bold black ceilings and crisp white walls.
  • Industrial aesthetics with exposed ductwork.
  • Clean lines and modern finishes that complement the high-tech nature of quantum computing.

2.Thoughtful Material Selection

To align with sustainability goals, we carefully selected materials for their low environmental impact.
  • Ceiling and Ducts: Black-painted, exposed ducting not only enhances the futuristic look but also supports efficient air circulation.
  • Partitions: Smooth glass for soundproofing while allowing visual connectivity.
  • Workstations and Walls: Light wooden textures to foster warmth and natural aesthetics in the workspaces.
  • Storage: Acrylic finish doors double as whiteboards for functionality.

1. Energy Analysis

Our journey began with an in-depth energy analysis of the space. We evaluated the energy consumption patterns to optimize the lab's operational efficiency. The lab design incorporated thermal insulation, optimized natural lighting, and energy-efficient fixtures. These measures ensure substantial energy savings while maintaining the necessary technical standards for a high-precision quantum lab.

Sustainability Measures

  • Incorporation of energy-efficient lighting and HVAC systems.
  • Use of recycled and locally sourced materials wherever possible.
  • Adaptive furniture to optimize resource usage over time.

Visual Aesthetic

The design evolved from the idea of a "future-ready laboratory," where every element speaks of innovation. The bold interplay of white and black elements, the exposed ceilings, and sleek glass partitions come together to create an inspiring workspace for the researchers and scientists at CQuICC.
