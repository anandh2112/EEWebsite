Featured Projects

Centre for Quantum Information, Communication and Computing Lab

- Space Design

Elements Energy designed India's first Quantum Computing Laboratory for CQuICC, blending energy efficiency with a futuristic aesthetic. The 10,000 sqft space features thermal insulation, optimized lighting, bold black ceilings, glass partitions, and industrial elements, ensuring innovation, sustainability, and operational efficiency.

Metalware Corporation - Energy Analysis

Elements Energy conducted a comprehensive energy analysis for Metalware Corporation in Noida, integrating BIM modeling, local climate assessment, and solar feasibility studies. We proposed a 65 kW solar PV system, optimizing daylight utilization, and long-term sustainability. Our recommendations enable ₹12 lakh annual savings, a 3-year payback period, and 114,800 kg CO₂ reduction.

Ecotech Factory - Energy Analysis

The Ecotech Factory in Greater Noida integrates a 265 kW solar PV system and advanced lighting to enhance sustainability. Generating 4,60,000-4,75,000 kWh annually, it saves ₹38 lakhs and offsets 423,200 kg CO₂. Optimized panel placement and daylight enhancements improve efficiency, ensuring compliance with lighting standards while reducing impact and long-term costs.
